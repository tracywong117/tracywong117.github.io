## AI Video Segment Cutter

Have you ever come across those captivating videos on platforms like YouTube, Twitter, or Instagram that count how many times certain words are spoken? These videos compile snippets of those word instances into one concise video. Now, with the power of AI, you can accomplish this task effortlessly in just a matter of minutes, without manual clipping.

OpenAI's Whisper model is a cutting-edge solution that can accurately transcribe speech into text. In fact, it boasts remarkable accuracy, potentially surpassing human capabilities in this field. Leveraging the capabilities of the Whisper base model, we have developed a Python program, the AI Video Segment Cutter, which rapidly generates subtitles for videos and extracts specific segments based on user-defined keywords.
